Styles and The Meanings

We will start with comfort first, the square toes broad or standard are tradionaly used in farming and types of work that are on uneven ground and in mud where you might need more stability and comfort. Where round toes are typically found in manufactoring for the comfort as well but the rounded front often help when you run into things let your foot glance off and be protected. Last we have the pointed boots, they come in all kinds of designs as these boots are mainly worn as a dress style boot, and are often have very elaborite styles of leather as well, like gator, ostrich, or even rattlesnake.
